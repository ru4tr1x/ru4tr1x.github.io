Discover Canyoning

This is a fun and enjoyable introduction to the joy of Canyoning with our expert guides. Learn abseiling and rope techniques and use them to discover ancient rock formations in a beautiful Scottish Glen. We learn how to safely jump, slide and climb down the course of the burn as we explore a 400million year old chasm of volcanic rock.

Discover Canyoning in Dollar Glen

"Dollar Glen is an ideal location for first time canyoners, here's why"


  It is very easily accessible with low comitment, this means you can get in and out at almost any spot along the way


  You can choose the action! Dollar Glen has a great footpath which is usefull for missing out the scary bits or perfect for spectators and photo's


  It has all the features we look for in a fun canyoning trip! Abseils, jumps, slides, stunning waterfalls and deep pools!!
