An Enchanting Morning

Meet your guide: Begin your adventure from your accommodation or meet us at Dollar Glen where an enchanting morning adventure awaits.

Learn fun new skills: Explore a 400-million-year-old chasm formed from volcanic rock. This ancient glen, blanketed in moss and ferns, brims with magic and wonder. The air is perfumed with the scent of wild garlic, while white-throated dippers, wagtails, and treecreepers observe as you navigate through waterfalls and natural swimming pools. This experience serves as the perfect introduction to canyoning, a pursuit that grants access to some of Scotland's most untouched wilds. Learn more about canyoning in Dollar Glen

Picnic Lunch: Following a morning filled with jumping, sliding, and abseiling into natural pools, relish a hearty picnic lunch in a picturesque setting, preparing you for further adventures in the afternoon.

Afternoon

Alva Glen: After the mornings action you'll be ready for a next level experience in Alva Glen. Sculpted from the same ancient basalt, Alva offers a deeper, more narrow, and challenging adventure. Revel in the stunning valley views as we ascend to the Smuggler's Cave and commence an exciting descent by abseiling into it. Those who venture down to the river might catch sight of the waterfall deep within the cave, marvelling at the adventures that lie ahead. Picture their astonishment as we exit this grand natural spectacle and continue on down the Alva Burn to discover the hidden treasures below. Laarn more about canyoning in Alva Glen

Relax and Reflect

Conclude an extraordinary day of action with some well deserved home-baked snacks, sharing photos and stories of bravery.
