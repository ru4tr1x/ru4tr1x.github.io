Arrival and Accomodation

Arrive at Brucefield Estate and immerse yourself in an environment where the warm, unmistakable scent of wood greets you. This luxurious Scottish retreat, nestled in woodlands, offers an escape from the everyday, blending the tranquility of nature with modern conveniences. Experience a unique stay that combines the best of both worlds: modern amenities set against the backdrop of Scotland's natural beauty. For a deeper dive into what Brucefield Schenbothies have to offer, including more about its balance of tradition and modernity, explore the Brucefield website

Morning
Explore the hidden wonders of Dollar Glen



{% include _images/logo.html %}

Meet your canyoning guide: Wake up to a delightful, locally-sourced welcome pack with sourdough bread, honey, & speciality tea.. Then, meet your guide and start your adventure right from your doorstep. After a brief transfer to Dollar Glen, an enchanting morning's adventure awaits.



Learn fun new skills: Practice New Skills in a Safe Place before exploring a 400-million-year-old chasm of volcanic rock. This ancient moss- and fern-covered glen is full of magic and wonder. Blooms of wild garlic fill the air, and white-throated dippers, wagtails, and treecreepers watch on as you navigate a series of waterfalls and wild swimming pools. This is an ideal introduction to canyoning, an adventure activity that accesses some of Scotland's wildest locations. Learn more about canyoning in Dollar Glen

Picnic Lunch: After a thrilling morning of jumping, sliding, and abseiling into wild swimming pools, enjoy a hearty picnic lunch in a scenic spot to set you up for an exhilarating afternoon exploring Alva Glen

Afternoon
Exhilarating Waterfall Challenge in Alva Glen

Alva Glen Adventure: After a Dollar Glen experience you're ready for the captivating beauty of Alva Glen. Sculpted from ancient basalt, Alva presents a more profound, narrower, and ultimately more committing challenge. As we ascend towards the Smugglers Cave, be prepared to be awestruck by the magnificent views of the valley unfurling beneath us. The adventure intensifies as we begin with an exhilarating abseil into the cave itself. Hikers venturing down to the river are often mesmerized by the sight of a rushing waterfall deep within the cave's heart, pondering the thrill of exploration that lies within. Imagine their astonishment as we emerge from this breathtaking natural marvel, continuing our journey down the Alva burn to discover the hidden wonders that lie below. Join us on this journey to explore the untamed beauty and mystery of Alva Glen, where each step unveils a story, and every turn promises an unforgettable adventure. Learn more about canyoning in Alva Glen

Day Two
A Wild Ride

Mountain Bike Adventure: Discover the Heart of Adventure in Clackmannanshire: Nestled in the shadow of the majestic Ochil Hills, Clackmannanshire beckons adventurers and explorers to Scotland's Wee County, a treasure trove of natural beauty and historical marvels.





Imagine embarking on a journey where ancient castles, serene nature reserves, and Roman hillforts unfold before you, all within a pedal's reach. The county's extensive cycle path system connects an array of attractions, from tranquil riverside rides to exhilarating mountain bike trails that dare you to test your limits. Find out more from Real World Riding
