Meet you guide: Start your adventure from your Accommodation and transfer to Dollar Glen where an enchanting mornings adventure awaits.

Learn fun new skills: in a safe place before exploring a 400 million year old chasm of Volcanic rock. This ancient moss and fern covered glen if full of magic and wonder. Blooms of Wild Garlic fill the air and white throated dippers, wag tails and tree creeper watch on as you take a series of waterfalls and wild swimming pools. This is an ideal introduction to canyoning, an adventure activity that accesses some of Scotlands wildest and locations.

Picnic LunchAfter a thrilling morning of jumping, sliding and abseiling into wild swimming pools enjoy a hearty picnic lunch in a scenic spot to set you up for an exhiarating afternoon exploring a next level Challenge.

Day Two

Alva Glen:A short transfer in we'll arive at Alva Glen. Carved from the same ancient basalt rock Alva forms a deeper, narrower and more commiting challenge. Magnificent views of the valley as we hike up to the Smugglers Cave and begin an exhilarating morning by abseiling into it. Hikers that climb down to the river often glimpse the rushing waterfall deep inside the cave and marvel at the adventure it could be to explore. Imagine there surprise as we emerge from this magnificent natural wonder and continue down the Alva burn to the hidden wonders below.
