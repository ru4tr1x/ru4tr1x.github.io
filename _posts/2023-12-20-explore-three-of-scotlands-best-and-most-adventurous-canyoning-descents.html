Scotland's canyoning scene boasts a spectacular and diverse array of canyons, each with its own unique features and challenges. These canyons are not only stunningly beautiful but also offer heart-pumping wild adventures in precious landscapes that need to be treasured and protected. We are excited to showcase three extraordinary canyons on the West Coast, each ideal for more daring adventurers for very different reasons. Ready for the next level of adventure? All are available to book with Scotland's Canyons. Perhaps one of these incredible locations will be your next canyoning adventure?





Eagles Canyon and the Black Linn at Glen Fyne

Embrace the excitement of Eagles Canyon, where on a clear day, you'll feel as if you're soaring over the hills. After a long, steep hike, the adventure begins with a thrilling descent of the Eas an Tuirc, a narrow slot that drops into the main chasm. The breathtaking views from this narrow slot provide a magnificent start to an epic day filled with exhilarating abseil challenges. The intricate rock formations on either side of the deep and narrow canyon occasionally provide just enough space to pass through. This adventure is ideal for our guests who are confident in the basic skills and ready for a big and challenging day of canyoning adventure.

{% include _gallery/eagles.html %}

At A Glance

Duration: Full Day
Hike in: Steep and challenging, 1-2 hours
Experience Required: Suitable for those who are confident and well-practiced in the basic skills and ready to take on the next level. High fitness level required!
Highlights: Exhilarating abseils in a dramatic chasm





Sax Canyon at Kinloch Hourn

Dive into the wild beauty of Sax Canyon. The Allt a' Choire Reidh, flowing into the secluded Loch Hourn, presents an off-the-beaten-path adventure, unveiling Scotland's epic natural wonders at every turn. On clear days, the jaw-dropping views add an extra thrill to this adventure, with a mix of abseils, jumps, and stunning scenery making it a total package. Perfect for a full-day trip or an adventurous overnight camping mission, this special canyon is a true hidden gem



At A Glance

Duration:Full Day
Experience Required: This canyon is suitable for strong swimmers only, where confident abseilers will relish the action of a sporty descent. High fitness levels are required.
Hike in: Steep, 1 - 1.5 hours
Highlights: Breathtaking views in a wild and wonderful setting





Grey Mare's Tail at Kinlochleven

The Grey Mare's Tail in Kinlochleven offers non-stop excitement, kicking off with one of our all-time favorites, a pulse-racing slide. On an action-packed descent, you will discover more blood-pumping slides, fun jumps, and majestic waterfalls. The grand finale is an adrenaline-spiking 50-meter abseil down the magnificent Grey Mare's Waterfall, capping off an exhilarating canyon descent.



At A Glance

Duration: Half Day
Experience Required: To truly enjoy all the featiure on this magnificent descent guests should be confident with more advanced abseiling challenges and have a strong head for heights.
Hike in: Easy, 30 minutes
Highlights: Action-packed adventure with an epic waterfall





Summary

These three canyons are recommended for experienced adventurers who have a good grasp of canyoning skills and understand what to expect from long adventurous descents.
