Schenbothies: A Romantic Hideaway Near Adventure


Just a short drive from Dollar and Alva Glen, Schenbothies offer a wild and idyllic retreat near some of our favourite canyoning spots. These eco-friendly cabins, styled in a blend of Scandinavian and Scottish design, are our premier accommodation recommendation for couples seeking both serenity and excitement. After a day of wild adventures in the ancient volcanic glens of the Ochils, retreat to your cosy bothy, nestled amidst ancient woodlands and less than an hour from Edinburgh or Glasgow. The perfect fusion of romance and adventure awaits you at Brucefield Estate.



Slackbrae: A Cosy Cottage with a Touch of Magic.


Nestled in the charming woodlands of Brucefield Estate, Slackbrae Cottage is a beautifully refurbished 18th-century haven, ideal for those seeking both comfort and adventure. With its sustainable design, underfloor heating, and a charming ceramic wood-burning stove, Slackbrae offers a cosy stay brimming with modern conveniences. An exemplary base for exploring the nearby Dollar and Alva Glen, Slackbrae is perfect for couples, families, or small groups (sleeps 4 + 1) embarking on our canyoning adventures, providing a magical, restful retreat with scenic views of ancient trees and local wildlife right from your window.



Transfers and multi day canyoning

For any private canyoning booking we'll pick you up and drop you off right from outside your accommodation at Brucefield Estate ensuring a seamless holiday of enchanting adventure.
