What we do

Scotland’s canyons are hidden and ancient temples. To go canyoning is to explore a special place. We go Swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We introduce specialised harnesses, carabiners and ropes then use them to reach deep pools and majestic waterfalls. We will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.

Who We are

This is us canyoning together in Alva Glen, our favourite Scottish Canyon. Diana, one year old Finn and Ruaridh are a family operator with experience from all over the world. Ask us about canyoning in New Zealand, Switzerland, Italy and Spain. We'd love to hear form you! Call Us

What we offer

We offer private canyoning for groups, couples or individulals in any canyon in scotland. We also offer fixed date events to join other canyoners in a group at a similar level. From nervous beginners to adrenaline hunters we make canyoning accessible to everyone!

Equipment

It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain only the highest standards of gear to keep you warm and secure.

Safety

If it isn't safe we won't do it. Powered by adventure logic we will never take unnecessary risks. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards, first aid training and incident management.

Experience

It's your experience! We measure our success from your satisfaction. You will always be given the choice to go and we will always be clear about the challenges ahead.
