What we do

Scotland's Canyons are hidden and ancient temples, to explore them is to discover a special place. Canyoning is swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We introduce specialised harnesses, carabiners and ropes and use them to discover deep pools and majestic waterfalls. We will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.

Who We are

We are a local family operator but we have experience from all over the world, we would love to tell you about canyoning in New Zealand, Switzerland and Catalonia. Now we are settling at home to explore and enjoy the beautiful canyons of Scotland. We love questions and would be delighted to share our knowledge and experience of canyoning and adventure sport.

What we offer

We run private canyoning and events. To make the most out of every trip we have coupled a bespoke service with an event schedule. You can come alone, bring your friends or sign up to meet like minded adventurers at a similar level.

Our Equipment

One of the easiest things to control on any adventure is the clothing and equipment you use. It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain the highest standards of gear to keep you warm and secure.

Your Safety

For us safety is more than a goal, it is a necessity of operation. If it isn't safe we won't do it. Powered by adventure logic we will never take unnecessary risks. We practise with our peers regularly in order to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and be prepared when things don't go to plan with relevant first aid training and incident management.

Your experience

We guide and lead in a manner that celebrates individuality. You will always have a choice to go and we will always be clear about the challenges ahead. We prioritise inclusivity so whether you join us for an event or book us privately we will work with your specific needs and requirements. You are unique and what you discover with us is entirely yours to take away.
