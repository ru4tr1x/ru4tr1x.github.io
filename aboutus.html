What we do

Scotland's Canyons are hidden and ancient temples, to explore them is to discover a special place. Canyoning is swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We introduce specialised harnesses, carabiners and ropes and use them to discover deep pools and majestic waterfalls. We will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.

Who We are

We are a family operator based in Fife, with experience from all over the world. We run regular canyoning events in the Ochil hills and offer private guiding anywhere is Scotland. We also enjoy visiting some of europe's best canyoning locations, running annual trips to the Alps and the Pyrenees. We would love to answer your questions and would be delighted to share our knowledge and experience of canyoning and adventure sport.
