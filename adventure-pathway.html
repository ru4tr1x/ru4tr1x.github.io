Learn Adventure Skills with Scotland's Canyons

The adventure pathway teaches the skills, knowledge and mindset that unlock unforgettable moments in any of Scotland's canyons. Through experiential learning, we'll help you accomplish your goals and build the confidence to go out and lead adventures safely.



★★★★★
True Adventure Starts Here

Learn exciting new skills in small groups with dedicated mentorship and support.


★ Learn the Skills
Canyoning skills empower true adventure. Quick to learn and fun to master, developing your skills is the key to unlocking safe and rewarding adventures. The basic skills are taught and used in every first-time experience. These same skills are valuable in even the most challenging situations. It's thrilling to develop and master these skills, building the experience and confidence needed for Scotland's fascinating canyoning adventures and beyond.

★ We Focus on Developing Skills in Real Situations

★ Fun and Relevant: Our training is not just enjoyable; its directly relevant to gaining valuable experience.

★ Active Learning: Learning by doing is a vibrant and engaging process that immerses you in the adventure.

★ Memorable Experiences: Each session leaves you with a story to tell, creating long-lasting and valuable memories of your newly acquired skills.


★ Master the Mindset
An adventurous mindset is a powerful tool that can help us make safe and confident choices in all aspects of life. When faced with a challenge, an adventure leader trusts practiced skills and uses knowledge and experience to make strong choices. Tasks that seem routine can become daunting when faced with adversity. Seeing the risk up close, it's clear understanding and focused thinking that help us maintain composure. Knowing what's achievable and comprehending the risks, coupled with the experience to manage them, illuminates our path forward. At this crucial moment, the key to reaching our goal is simply having the confidence to follow that path.


★ Love the Adventure
Adventure is inherent in us, and canyoning invigorates the instinct to explore. Every moment of discovery is unique, and the joy of experiencing Scotland's wild and beautiful canyons is infectious.

Our Commitment to Safety


For each adventure you will learn to qualify yourself through relevant and recent experience, advice from your mentors and diligent observations, dynamically assessing the risk involved in any given situation. We only embark on safe, legal and enjoyable adventures and if we reasonably assume that an activity is not all of these things then we won't do it.




Equipment

It's natural that as you develop your understanding of canyoning and the equipment involved you will want your own. Over time we will help you choose and source appropriate equipment. In the mean time you are always welcome to hire our premium quality equipment for use in your training sessions. It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain only the highest standards of gear to keep our guests warm and secure.




Discover the Thrill of Canyoning in Scotland's Top Locations


From the rugged, sandstone cliffs of Ardessie Falls in the North to the cascading beauty of Gray Mare's Tail in the West, each canyon offers a unique adventure. Whether it's exploring the ancient volcanic gorges of Alva and Dollar Glen or embracing the adrenaline rush at Bruar's famous jumping spots, Scotland's diverse canyons are perfect for enthusiasts of all skill levels.
