Looking for things to do in Scotland?

Try canyoning! We offer fun outdoor activity and true adventure. Scotlands Canyons offer one of Scotlands best adventures, fun for adults, great for couples and a real Scottish canyoning experience. Located just 45 minutes from Edinburgh, 30 minutes from Central Fife and less than 1 hour from Glasgow you can find some of the best canyoning locations in the Ochil hills.
