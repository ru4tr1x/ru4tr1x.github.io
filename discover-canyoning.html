Join us for an unforgettable journey through the enchanting landscapes of Scotland's Dollar Burn, where each step reveals the timeless beauty of a 400 million-year-old volcanic landscape. Experience canyoning in a magical setting that blends ancient wonders with exhilarating adventure.

Discover Dollar Glen: Scotland's Beginner-Friendly Canyoning Paradise

Dollar Glen is near Edinburgh, Glasgow and Fife and is the perfect canyoning destination for beginners and enthusiasts alike. With easy access and the option to adjust your experience along the way, it caters to all levels of adventure seekers.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Join us on a 4-hour adventure that will include everything from waterfall abseils and fun jumps to natural slides and wild swimimming pools. Expect an exhilarating descent lasting 2-3 hours, filled with unforgettable moments.

Learning and Exploring Safely


★A Warm Welcome: Begin your canyoning adventure at the Dollar Glen Car Park. Here, you'll meet your guide and group, and gear up with quality shoes, harnesses, and helmets.


★Smart Preparation: Learn and practise essential canyoning skills in a safe environment before descending the Dollar Burn. We will take the time to learn basic skills that will boost confidence and help us enjoy the action

.

★The Action: We'll set off to descend the canyon at your pace. Small groups mean that we can focus our attention on leading an adventure that fits. We'll be clear about the challenges ahead and, when appropriate, offer a choice of action. Dollar Glen is the ideal location because you can get in and out along the way.


★ Relax and Reflect: Conclude your journey with a delightful touch. After descending, we gather back at the meeting point for home-baked snacks and drinks. It's a chance to relax, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow canyoners.


Staying Safe


★Our Commitment to Safety: If it isn't safe, we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards. We understand that every adventure is unique, and our approach to safety reflects that.

Heightened awareness and skillful observations are fundamental to our operations, skills honed through years of experience. We prioritize constant vigilance, as safety is a non-negotiable aspect of all our activities. To ensure the highest safety standards, our guides regularly undergo specialised training, tailored to both the specific location and activity. This commitment to safety is grounded in up-to-date and relevant expertise, ensuring the best possible experience for our guests.
