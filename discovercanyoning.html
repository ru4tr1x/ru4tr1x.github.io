Discover Canyoning in Scotland

Join us for an unforgettable journey through the enchanting landscapes of Scotland's Dollar Burn, where each step reveals the timeless beauty of a 400 million-year-old volcanic landscape. Experience canyoning in a magical setting that blends ancient wonders with exhilarating adventure.



Discover Dollar Glen: A Beginner-Friendly Canyoning Paradise

Dollar Glen is the perfect canyoning destination for beginners and enthusiasts alike. With easy access and the option to adjust your experience along the way, it caters to all levels of adventure seekers.

Map and Directions

Canyoning in Dollar Glen in Clackmannanshire is Near Edinburgh, Glasgow, Stirling and Fife.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Join us on a 4-hour adventure that will include everything from wet abseils and fun jumps to exploring stunning waterfalls and deep pools. Expect an exhilarating descent lasting 2-3 hours, filled with unforgettable moments.

Kickstarting Your Adventure: Meeting and Preparation

★ Ready, Set, Go!: Begin your canyoning adventure at the Dollar Glen Car park. Here, you'll meet your guide and group, and gear up with quality shoes, harnesses, and helmets.

Learning and Exploring Safely

★ Skill Development & Thrilling Discoveries: Learn and practice essential canyoning skills in a safe environment before descending the Dollar Burn. Your adventure includes navigating hidden waterfalls and pristine wild swimming pools, on a thrilling adventure.

★ Our Commitment to Safety: If it isn't safe we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards, first aid training and incident management. We understand that every adventure is unique, and our approach to safety reflects that. Heightened awareness and skillful observations are fundamental to our operations, skills honed through years of experience. We prioritize constant vigilance, as safety is a non-negotiable aspect of all our activities. To ensure the highest safety standards, our guides regularly undergo specialised training, tailored to both the specific location and activity. This commitment to safety is grounded in up-to-date and relevant expertise, ensuring the best possible experience for our guests.

Unwind with Refreshments After the Adventure

★ Relax and Reflect: Conclude your journey with a delightful touch. After descending, we gather back at the meeting point for home-baked snacks and drinks. It's a chance to relax, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow canyoners.
