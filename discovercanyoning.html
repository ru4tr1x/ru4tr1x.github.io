Discover Canyoning

This is a fun introduction to the joy of Canyoning. Learn how to safely jump, slide and climb down the course of the Dollar Burn as you explore a 400 million year old chasm of volcanic rock.

Location: Dollar Glen

double click on the map to search.

"Dollar Glen is an ideal location for first time canyoners"


  It is easily accessible with low commitment, this means you can get in and out along the way


  You can choose the action to suit your level! Dollar Glen has all the features we look for in a fun canyoning trip!


  Discover wet abseils, fun jumps, cool slides, stunning waterfalls and deep pools


Your Adventure at a Glance

Discover Canyoning takes a total of 4 hours with a typical decent lasting 2-3 hours.

Welcome

Each tour kicks off with a welcome meeting in Dollar Glen Car park where you will meet the group and your guide.

Action

We introduce and practice the skills required in a safe place before descending the Dollar Burn. We discover hidden waterfalls and wild swimming pools by sliding, jumping and abseiling.

Snacks

Following the descent we will return to the meeting point for home baked snacks and drinks provided by us.

Equipment

It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain only the highest standards of gear to keep you warm and secure.

Safety

If it isn't safe we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards, first aid training and incident management. All our trips are supported by Pharos Response.

Covid-19

We are very fortunate that our trips involve a small numbers of participants, usually from one or two households and take place entirely in the outdoors. We are very careful to follow current government guidelines and regulations.
