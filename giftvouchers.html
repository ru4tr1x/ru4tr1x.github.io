The Ultimate Gift Experience

Our gift certificates unlock true adventure in some of Scotland's most stunning locations. Our personal approach to guiding ensures that our guests have a genuine and unique experience tailored to their every requirement.

Who are gift certificates for?

☆ For a friend or relative
☆ For a couple

You can order a gift certificate for up to 6 guests. We have no minimum number to run an adventure so you can be confident that your gift will be an unforgetable experience on the perfect day. Guests must be over 18 years of age or accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

The Experience

All our adventures are lead by expert canyoning guides and run with small numbers or private groups. This guarantees a personal touch and an adventure to suit.

Duration

Our adventures usually take at least half a day with a typical decent lasting 2-3 hours.

The Welcome

Each tour kicks off with a welcome meeting at a place and time confirmed by the guide.

The Action

Following a practice of the skills required guests descend the canyon discovering hidden waterfalls and wild swimming pools.

The Snacks

Unless arranged otherwise the group return to the meeting point for home baked snacks and drinks provided by us.
