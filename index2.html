About Us

Our Mission

A true adventure is to explore the unknown with a purpose to discover. To explore a canyon is to discover a special place. Canyoning is to swim, climb and jump as we navigate on of nature's temples. We follow the river through the rocky chasm and discover deep pools, waterfalls, wildlife and magical beauty carved by the ancient power of natures architect. Scotland has many of these places to explore. We aim to help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the journey.

Who We are

We are a small operator based in Fife un run regular canyoning events in the Ochil hills. Our team have gained experience from all over the world from as far afield as New Zealand, India and Zambia as well as some of europe's best canyoning in Switzerland and the Pyrenees. We enjoy discovering new canyons at home and aim to offer our guiding and equipment hire services anywhere in Scotland. We would love to hear your questions and would be delighted to share our knowledge and experience of canyoning and adventure sport from all over the world.
