What we do

To explore a canyon is to discover a special place. Canyoning is swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We'll teach you how to use harneses, caribiners and ropes to discover deep pools and majestic waterfalls. Canyons are hidden temples of the natural world, carved in stone by the insessant power of water, it's ancient architect. Scotland has many of these wonderous places to explore, we will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.

Who We are

We are a small family operator based in Fife. We run regular canyoning events in the Ochil hills and offer private guiding and equipment hire services. Our guides have experience from all over the world, as far afield as New Zealand, India and Zambia. We also offer tours to some of europe's best canyoning locations, with anual trips to Ticono Switzerland and the Catalonian Pyrenees. Most of all we enjoy discovering new canyons at home where we offer our guiding and equipment hire services anywhere in Scotland. We would love to hear your questions and would be delighted to share our knowledge and experience of canyoning and adventure sport.
