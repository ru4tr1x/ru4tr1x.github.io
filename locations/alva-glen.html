Alva Glen: Exhilarating canyoning adventure

Alva Glen in Clackmannanshire is within an hour's drive from Edinburgh, Glasgow, Dundee, Perth, and Fife. It's the perfect destination for adventure seekers looking for a wild and exhilarating experience, abseiling inside and through waterfalls. This is a committing descent, better suited to those with strong abseiling skills who have previous canyoning experience.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Join us for a half-day adventure that encounters challenging waterfall abseils on an exhilarating decent of the Alva Burn. Anticipate a thrilling and commiting challenge, in a magnificent, deep, steep and narrow slot canyon

Learning and Exploring Safely


★A Warm Welcome: Begin your canyoning adventure at the Alva Glen Car Park. Here, you'll meet your guide and group, and gear up with our quality shoes, harnesses, and helmets.


★Smart Preparation: Re-visit and if necessary practice the essential canyoning skills in a safe environment before descending The Alva Burn. Along the way, we will take the time to describe the challenges ahead and revisit the skills required preparing you to enjoy the action.


★The Action: We'll descend the canyon at your pace. Private canyoning means that we can focus our attention on leading an adventure that's uniquely yours. We'll be clear about the challenges ahead and if possible offer a choice of action. Alva Glen has commiting sections that are inaccessible, this mean there is no way out other than down and through. We’ll take this into consideration when planning and preparing for a fun and fulfilling adventure


★ Relax and Reflect: Conclude your journey with a delightful touch. After the descent, we gather back at the meeting point for home-baked snacks and drinks. It's a chance to relax, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow canyoners.
