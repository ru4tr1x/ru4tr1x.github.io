The Bruar Water: Scotland's All Action Canyoning Paradise

Bruar Canyon in Perthshire is easily accessible from the A9 and lies near Pitlochry, Blair Atholl, and Aberfeldy. It's the perfect destination for adventure seekers looking for a wild, action-packed experience full of jumps and slides. With its easy access and the flexibility to tailor your experience along the way, it caters to all levels of adventure enthusiasts.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Join us for a full-day adventure that encompasses everything from waterfall abseils and exhilarating jumps to natural slides and serene wild swimming pools. Anticipate a thrilling descent, filled with action-packed moments both before and after a delightful picnic lunch.

Learning and Exploring Safely


★A Warm Welcome: Begin your canyoning adventure at the House of Bruar Car Park. Here, you'll meet your guide and group, and gear up with quality shoes, harnesses, and helmets.


★Smart Preparation: Learn and practise essential canyoning skills in a safe environment before descending The Bruar Water. Along the way, we will take the time to learn basic skills that will boost confidence and help us enjoy the action.


★The Action: We'll descend the canyon at your pace. Private canyoning means that we can focus our attention on leading an adventure that's uniquely yours. We'll be clear about the challenges ahead and, when appropriate, offer a choice of action. Bruar is a great location for beginners because you can get in and out along the way.


★Lunch: We'll carry a hearty picnic lunch with us to enjoy in a magnificent setting along the way.


★ Relax and Reflect: Conclude your journey with a delightful touch. After the descent, we gather back at the meeting point for home-baked snacks and drinks. It's a chance to relax, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow canyoners.

Staying Safe


★Our Commitment to Safety: If it isn't safe, we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards. We understand that every adventure is unique, and our approach to safety reflects that.

Heightened awareness and skillful observations are fundamental to our operations, skills honed through years of experience. We prioritize constant vigilance, as safety is a non-negotiable aspect of all our activities. To ensure the highest safety standards, our guides regularly undergo specialised training, tailored to both the specific location and activity. This commitment to safety is grounded in up-to-date and relevant expertise, ensuring the best possible experience for our guests.
