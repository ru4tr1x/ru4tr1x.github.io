The Grey Mare's Tail: Thrilling Adventure Experience

An absolute classic, The Grey Mare's Tail is packed full of mind-blowing features. This canyon is better suited to those with practised skills. If you've been with us a few times before, or if you've started your journey on our Adventure Pathway, your skills and experience will enable you to fully appreciate and enjoy the spectacular challenges of this magnificent canyon. Along the way, you'll encounter some of our all-time favourite slides, exhilarating jumps, and an awe-inspiring descent of the 70-metre high Grey Mare's Waterfall. The final abseil is 50 metres high, making it one of Scotland's most thrilling waterfall abseils.

Get Directions to Grey Mare's Tail Waterfall, Kinlochleven

This canyoning adventure at Kinlochleven, in Lochaber, the Scottish Highlands, is near Fort William, Glencoe, and Ben Nevis. It's an incredible addition to any adventure holiday in Scotland and a must-do experience for thrill-seekers and canyoning enthusiasts alike.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Join us for a full-day adventure that includes massive waterfall abseils, wild slides, and exhilarating jumps in the Grey Mare's Tail canyon. Anticipate a thrilling and committing challenge with significant exposure to heights.

Learning and Exploring Safely


★ A Warm Welcome: Begin your canyoning adventure at the Grey Mare'e Waterfall Car Park. Here, you'll meet your guide, and gear up with our quality shoes, harnesses, and helmets.


★ Smart Preparation: Re-visit and if necessary practice the essential canyoning skills in a safe environment before descending The Grey Mare's Tail. Along the way, we will take the time to describe the challenges ahead and revisit the skills required preparing you to enjoy the action.


★ The Action: We'll descend the canyon at your pace. Private canyoning means that we can focus our attention on leading an adventure that's uniquely yours. We'll be clear about the challenges ahead and if possible offer a choice of action. The Grey Mares tail has commiting sections that are inaccessible, this mean there is no way out other than down and through. We'll take this into consideration when planning and preparing for a fun and fulfilling adventure


★ The Lunch: We'll prepair a hearty picnic lunch to enjoy in a scenic spot along the way.


★ Relax and Reflect: Conclude your journey with a delightful touch. After the descent, we gather back at the meeting point for home-baked snacks and drinks. It's a chance to relax, share stories, and enjoy the camaraderie of fellow canyoners.
