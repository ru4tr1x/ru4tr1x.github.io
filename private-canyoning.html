Discover the Thrill of Canyoning in Scotland's Top Locations


From the rugged, sandstone cliffs of Ardessie Falls in the North to the cascading beauty of Gray Mare's Tail in the West, each canyon offers a unique adventure. Whether it's exploring the ancient volcanic gorges of Alva and Dollar Glen or embracing the adrenaline rush at Bruar's famous jumping spots, Scotland's diverse canyons are perfect for canyoning enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Your Adventure at a Glance

☆ Duration

Choose from a half-day adventure lasting 4 hours or a full-day experience spanning 6 to 8 hours, complete with a delightful picnic lunch.


☆ A Warm Welcome

Start your adventure with a friendly welcome. Your guide will show you the canyoning gear, chat about staying safe, and happily answer all your questions, getting you all set for the exciting action ahead.


☆ Smart Preparation

Get a head start on your canyoning adventure by learning and practicing essential skills in a safe setting. We'll dedicate time to teach or brush up on the necessary techniques before we begin our descent. This approach not only boosts confidence but also ensures we all enjoy the thrills safely.


☆ Adventure Awaits

In all our adventures, we'll take you through breathtaking waterfalls and wild swimming pools, unveiling the canyon's natural beauty with each step. We'll clearly outline the upcoming obstacles and encourage you to apply your skills to meet the challenges. Throughout, we'll support you in making well-informed decisions, ensuring you have the most rewarding and enjoyable experience possible.


☆ Time to Chill and Chat

Wrap up your adventure with some homemade snacks and drinks back where we started. It's the perfect time to relive the day's best bits, share some laughs, and check out the photos from our unforgettable journey.
