Discover the Exclusive Experience of Private Canyoning

Private canyoning offers a unique, tailored adventure where every detail is focused on providing you with an unforgettable experience. Your Adventure at a Glance

☆ Duration

Choose from a half-day adventure lasting 4 hours or a full-day experience spanning 6 to 8 hours, complete with a delightful picnic lunch.

☆ The Welcome

Begin your journey with a warm welcome meeting. Here, your guide will introduce the canyoning equipment, discuss safety protocols, and answer any queries, setting the stage for your adventure.

☆ The Action

Delve into an exciting hidden world as we hone essential canyoning skills. Your path will lead you through spectacular waterfalls and serene wild swimming pools, each step unveiling the natural beauty of the canyon.

☆ The Refreshment

Conclude your experience with homemade snacks and refreshing beverages back at our meeting point. This is the perfect moment to relax and share photos from your thrilling canyoning journey.


Discover the Thrill of Canyoning in Scotland's Top Locations

From the rugged, sandstone cliffs of Ardessie Falls in the North to the cascading beauty of Gray Mare's Tail in the West, each canyon offers a unique adventure. Whether it's exploring the ancient volcanic gorges of Alva and Dollar Glen or embracing the adrenaline rush at Bruar's famous jumping spots, Scotland's diverse canyons are perfect for enthusiasts of all skill levels.


Book Your Canyoning Adventure: Live Availability

Available throughout the year, our private canyoning experiences can be tailored to your schedule. Contact us anytime to start planning your adventure. We offer guidance on weather conditions and assist in selecting the ideal canyon location for your experience.
