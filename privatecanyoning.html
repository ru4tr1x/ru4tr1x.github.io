What is Private Canyoning?

More and more people are choosing to go private canyoning because private trips are more personal, flexible and focused entirely on delivering the experience that is right for you. We will plan your adventure in a way that suits you and deliver it in a style that makes the most out of your time.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Duration

Private Canyoning takes a total of 4 hours with a typical decent lasting 2-3 hours.

The Beggining

Each tour kicks off with a welcome meeting at a place and time prearranged between you and your guide.

The Middle

Following a practice of the skills required we will descend the canyon discovering hidden waterfalls and wild swimming pools.

The End

Unless arranged otherwise we return to the meeting point for home baked snacks and drinks provided by us.

Price

£50 per guest + £180 per guide. We use minimum one guide for every 6 guests

How to book

Simply get in touch, you can enquire now, Give us a call , send us an email  or message us on whastapp. 

Equipment

It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain only the highest standards of gear to keep you warm and secure.

Safety

If it isn't safe we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards, first aid training and incident management.

Experience

It's your experience! We measure our success from your satisfaction. You will always be given the choice to go and we will always be clear about the challenges ahead.
