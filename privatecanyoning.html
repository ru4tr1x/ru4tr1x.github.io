What is Private Canyoning?

Private canyoning means that you will have exclusive attention from our guiding team. Running private tours means that we can be more personal and deliver the experience that is right for you.

How much does it cost?

Our pricing stucture means that Private Canyoning is affordable for one and reasonably discounted for a group. We charge £180 per guide + £50 per guest

What is canyoning?

Scotland's Canyons are hidden and ancient temples, to explore them is to discover a special place. Canyoning is swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We introduce specialised harnesses, carabiners and ropes then use them to discover deep pools and majestic waterfalls. We will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.
