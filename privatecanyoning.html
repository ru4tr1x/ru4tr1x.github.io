What is Private Canyoning?

More and more people are choosing to go private canyoning because private trips are more personal, flexible and focused entirely on delivering the experience that is right for you. We will plan your adventure in a way that suits you and deliver it in a style that makes the most out of your time.

Your Adventure at a Glance

Duration

Our adventures take either a half day (4 hours) or full day (6 - 8 hours) with a picnic lunch included

The Welcome

Your adventure begins with a scheduled welcome meeting, where your guide will introduce you to the equipment and provide an opportunity to prepare and ask any questions you may have.

The Action

Embark on an exhilarating journey as we practice the required skills before descending the canyon, uncovering hidden waterfalls and encountering stunning wild swimming pools.

The Snacks

After the adventure, unless arranged otherwise, we return to the meeting point where you can enjoy delicious home-baked snacks and refreshing drinks. It's also a perfect time to share and exchange photos of your unforgettable experience.

Availability

We can run private canyoning on any day of the year and you may contact us at any time to start planning. We will advise you on the weather conditions and help you choose the most suitable location. Our most popular canyons have live availability so you may simply choose your prefered date and book online.

Equipment

It's more fun canyoning when you are comfortable so we use and maintain only the highest standards of gear to keep you warm and secure.

Safety

If it isn't safe we won't do it. We practise regularly to maintain the highest standards, first aid training and incident management. All our trips are supported by Pharos Response.
