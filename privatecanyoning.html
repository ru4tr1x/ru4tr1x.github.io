What is canyoning?

Scotland's Canyons are hidden and ancient temples, to explore them is to discover a special place. Canyoning is swimming, climbing, jumping and sliding down a stream as it plunges through a rocky chasm. We introduce specialised harnesses, carabiners and ropes and use them to discover deep pools and majestic waterfalls. We will help you discover Scotlands Canyons, the joy of canyoning and possibly something new about yourself along the way.
